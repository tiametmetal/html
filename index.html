Ascetic Eventide

Pure Wrath unleashes a wonderfull atmospheric album
with multiple levels of emotion that will keep you
entertained for the entire album.

Ruin

Melodic black metal at it's finest
is displayed in Cor Scorpii's Ruin.
A dynamic album with plenty of diversity
to satisfy the most demanding of metal fans.

Expedition:Darkness

Rimfrost's fusion of heavy and black metal
will be sure too knock your socks off and
have you coming back for more.

Viperous

Synthesizers provide atmosphere to Vredehammer's aggressive,
yet groove inducing offering.

The Eldritch Realm

Parius will take you on a wacky and playful trip through the Eldritch Realm.
A concept album inspired by the horror genre that at times feels so
lighthearted that it could be used to playout a Scooby Doo cartoon.

Expanding Senses

What Darkane brings to the table is a very unique
take to the melodic metal scene. Mixing in different vocal styles,
various amounts of tempo changes, and unconventional song structures
that will give you plenty to focus on while enjoying this gem.

Natural Born Chaos

Where Wages of Sin is the package, Natural Born Chaos is the bow.
Soilwork pair with Devin Townsend to unleash a truly epic
and melodic masterpiece.

Wages of Sin

Arch Enemy charges forward with Wages of Sin,
releasing aggressive, melodic and memorable tracks
wrapped inside a cohesive package.

Slaughter of the Soul

At the Gates deliver a highly aggressive album
with plenty of melody sprinkled in to have you humming along.

The Jester Race

One of Gothenburg's great treasures,
and one of In flames' best works in our opinion.
An album that flows consistantly and that
will surely have you move along to the beautiful melodies.
